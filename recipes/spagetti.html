Spagetti

The best spagetti you've ever had! your moma gonna want some!

This delicious and nutricious recipe can serve up to 8 people.

this recipe is going to make you feel from the motherland.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 box Spagetti
  • oregano
  • basil
  • garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  • In a deep suace pan put water to boil and set spagetti until tender
  • Put tomato paste, oregano, basil, gralic and 1 cup water in a blender and blend well for 2 minutes
  • In a deep sauce pan empty blender content with some olive oild.
  • Bring to simmer.
  • Serve spagetti and add suace
