Lasagna

The best lasagna you've ever had!

This delicious and nutricious recipe can serve up to 8 people.

If you are feeling brave add some paprika chile and spice it up a bit and they will remember you forever!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 box Pasta for lasagna
  • 1 pint Ricota cheese
  • 1 lbs of Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps:

  • In a baking pan lay one sheet oflasagna pasta
  • Spread tomato paste and ricota cheese
  • Add ground beef
  • Repeat two more times for a total of three layers
  • Add salt and pepper to taste
  • Set oven to 350 degrees F and put lasagna in for 1 hour or until golden brown.
